Daily Wellness

Small habits, meaningful change

Science-backed micro-practices you can weave into everyday life.

🌬️

Box Breathing

Inhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec → Exhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec. Repeat 4 cycles to calm your nervous system in under 2 minutes.

📝

Morning Journaling

Write 3 things you're grateful for and 1 intention each morning. This practice rewires neural pathways toward positivity over time.

🚶

10-Minute Movement

A brisk 10-minute walk releases endorphins, reduces cortisol, and improves focus. No gym required — just fresh air and intention.

😴

Sleep Consistency

Going to bed and waking at the same time (even weekends) is the single most impactful sleep habit for mental health.
